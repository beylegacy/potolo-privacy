Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2026

Po Tolo is a fully offline app. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. Your location is used only on your device to calculate sunrise and sunset times — it never leaves your phone.

About This Policy

Po Tolo ("the App") is developed and published by Bey Legacy LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Location Data

Po Tolo requests access to your device's GPS location for one purpose only: to calculate accurate local sunrise and sunset times so we can compute your precise planetary hours.

  • Location data is processed entirely on your device
  • Location data is never transmitted to any server
  • Location data is never stored beyond the current app session
  • Location data is never shared with any third party

You may deny location permission at any time in your device settings. Without location access, the app will not be able to calculate accurate planetary hours for your area.

No Account Required

Po Tolo requires no account creation, email address, phone number, or any personal information to download or use the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, process, or store any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Browsing history or app activity
  • Payment information

Third-Party Services

Po Tolo does not integrate any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or tracking tools. The app functions entirely with on-device calculations.

Children's Privacy

Po Tolo does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that collect personal data from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
